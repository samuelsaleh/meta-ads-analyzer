## Slide 2: Executive Summary
**Heading:** Notion deployed 150 ads in France with 35 unique creative variations

**Key Points:**
- Campaign focused on "3 Months Free Notion Business" promotional offer targeting enterprise users

- All ads ran simultaneously on Facebook and Instagram platforms

## Slide 10: Key Takeaways and Recommendations
**Heading:** Notion's France campaign reveals systematic testing approach for enterprise acquisition

**Strategic Insights:**

1. **High-Volume Testing:** 150 ads with 35 unique creatives indicates data-driven optimization strategy
2. **Value Anchoring:** "$12,000 of Free Notion" creates strong perceived value for enterprise buyers
3. **AI Positioning:** Consistent emphasis on AI capabilities aligns with market demand for automation tools
4. **Platform Focus:** Facebook and Instagram dual-platform approach maximizes B2B reach
5. **Conversion Simplicity:** Single CTA ("Get offer") reduces decision friction

**Recommendation:** Monitor Notion's creative evolution and landing page strategies for competitive intelligence updates.
